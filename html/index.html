Our Band

The St. Catherine Brass Band is a very unique tool for music ministry and we approach it wholeheartedly and seriously. The Band currently consists of approximately 35 members; men and women with a passion for music. It comprises an array of musical instruments, predominantly brass which includes, Trumpets, Saxophones (Alto, Soprano & Tenor), Baritones, Trombones, Bass Drum, Snares, Cymbals, Keyboards and a Bass Guitar.
